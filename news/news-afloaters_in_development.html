Can't believe the rumors were right! Afloaters really are in development, with a lot of work we are proud to present you some information about the game.

This development journey will be fascinating for both us and you! We plan on releasing this game somewhere around late 2027.

Afloaters will have an immersive story

Afloaters will include a wonderful and immersive story. We will try to add at least 5 chapters to this game.

Afloaters building system

Grid-based building system with an immersive crafting, researching and building. The game will have a new way on bringing creativity and productivity together.

Eating, drinking, stayin' alive

Afloaters will have a hunger, thirst as well as health bar. There will be multiple ways to get food like, fishing, hunting down creatures or try to starve and maybe you will recieve a buff or you will be doomed...

Afloaters will have multiplayer

You don't have to be alone. Using Steam Services we will be able for you and your friends to play together, able to survive, hunt down creatures and finish the story together.

Important Notice

These information given you might be changed or not implemented. We will inform you about these changes and the release date also might be delayed.

Afloaters Secrets

The ocean has its deepest secrets too. Explore the water beneath you in Afloaters and reveal the mystery yourself.

Quietly stalking the water and caves in search of rare species, wanting a fight to prove its strength.
